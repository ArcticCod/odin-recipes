Ingredients

  • Elbow Pasta
  • Velveeta
  • Ground Beef
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Sharp Cheddar

Steps

  1. Cook Beef
  2. Cook Pasta
  3. Melt cheese in pot
  4. Combine all ingredients
  5. Enjoy!
